# Modern Learning Techniques

Education is no longer confined to the classroom. Online platforms and interactive tools are making learning accessible to everyone.

## Online Courses

Platforms like Coursera and Udemy offer courses on almost any subject, taught by experts from around the world.

## Gamification

Adding game-like elements to learning can increase engagement and retention. Apps like Duolingo are perfect examples of this.
