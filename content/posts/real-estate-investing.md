# Investing in Commercial Real Estate

Commercial real estate can be a lucrative investment, but it comes with its own set of challenges and risks.

## Location, Location, Location

Just like residential real estate, the location of a commercial property is the most critical factor in its success.

## Understanding Leases

Commercial leases are complex. It's essential to understand the terms, including rent escalations, maintenance responsibilities, and lease duration.
